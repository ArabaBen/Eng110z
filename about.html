Hi, welcome to mediable. This is a group project focued on the effect technology has on people and their daily lives. we, the authors are: Kevin Joy, Araba O., Zach Bach, Fresley.

we hope that this website serves as a valuable source for our readers.

Our project topic focuses on the talking points of Social media's negative and positive impact on mental health.

Why it's worth sharing This information is important because it will make people aware of the negative effects that social media has on our mental health and might convince people to use it less.

We see our idea persuading our targeted audience because it will bring awareness of how social media is affecting us mentaly. It is important for people to know about these effects because if people learn about them and stop using excessive amounts of social media it will have a positive impact on their mental health and their lives as a whole.
